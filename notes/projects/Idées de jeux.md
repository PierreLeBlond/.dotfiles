## Clapping

## Platform/guitar hero/trumpet

## Platform

- One huge level, mostly vertical, where everything is reachable, given you do not die
- Bioshock inspired system, where you "clear" areas, which give you the sensation that what was once uncharted territory is now yours.
    - First the area is filled with monster, with scripted encounters
    - You then clean the area, unlocking shortcuts, event to others areas
    - The environment should reflect your passage, e.g. demolished building after a boss fight.
    - After a full clear, some monsters might still appears randomly to keep the place alive.
- Everything you do is persistent !
